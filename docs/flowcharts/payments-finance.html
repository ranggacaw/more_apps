Payments and Finance

Billing and Reports

Payments are the operational ledger; finance reports are cash-basis.

The active staff workflow primarily creates internal package invoices and treatment handoffs, while `PaymentController` retains Midtrans consultation and package checkout logic. Finance read access is limited to verified `super_admin` and `doctor`; mutations are limited to verified `super_admin`.

payments

Ledger Rows

`payments` stores consultation, package, and consultation-treatment billing with `midtrans` or `internal` providers.

webhook

Midtrans Authority

Provider callbacks validate signature, order, amount, and outcome before mutating payment, booking, slot, and package state.

internal

Staff Billing

Doctor-created internal package invoices and treatment handoffs wait for admin collection finalization.

p&l

Profit and Loss

Paid payments, returns, HPP, and operating expenses produce gross profit and net income.

balance

Balance Sheet

Cash and retained earnings derive from cumulative net income; manual entries add assets, equity, and liabilities.

controls

Finance Edits

Super admins adjust paid payment returns/HPP and manage operating expenses and balance-sheet entries.

Only the webhook route is active from `PaymentController` today.

The codebase still includes patient checkout, package catalog/init, and local simulation methods, but `routes/web.php` currently exposes only `POST /payment/webhook` from `PaymentController`.

Provider Callback

Midtrans success, pending, and failure are webhook-authoritative.

The webhook validates provider signature, payment provider, amount, and outcome before locking rows and applying consultation or package side effects.

Midtrans Webhook Lifecycle

Payloads are appended into `payload.latest` and `payload.history` for each outcome.

Retained Logic

Consultation checkout uses locked slots and a fixed fee.

Although not currently routed, the retained consultation checkout logic prepares a pending Midtrans payment for a locked slot and confirms the booking only after provider success.

Retained Logic

Package checkout consumes qualifying consultation credit.

Package checkout requires an eligible paid consultation credit tied to a completed qualifying consultation. Zero-balance purchases activate internally; remaining-balance purchases wait for Midtrans success.

Staff Collection

Internal package invoices and treatment handoffs are admin-finalized.

Doctor completion creates pending internal records. Package invoice finalization activates user packages; treatment payment finalization only marks the billing handoff as collected.

Report

Profit and loss is cash-basis from paid payments.

The P and L report includes paid payments in the selected date range, manual returns, HPP, and operating expenses. Pending internal treatment handoffs are listed separately and excluded from paid revenue.

Doctors and super admins can read the page; only super admins see mutation controls.

Statement

Balance sheet uses cumulative net income plus manual entries.

The simplified managerial balance sheet calculates retained earnings and cash from cumulative net income, then combines manual asset, equity, and liability rows.

Balance Sheet Calculation

Variance is total assets minus total equity and liabilities.

Super Admin

Finance mutations are restricted to verified super admins.

Super admins can adjust paid payment return and HPP amounts, create or update operating expenses, and create, update, or soft-delete manual balance-sheet entries.

Finance Mutation Boundary

Doctors can read finance reports but cannot mutate finance data.
