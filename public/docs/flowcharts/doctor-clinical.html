Doctor Clinical

Doctor Workspaces

Doctors complete clinical work and billing handoffs.

Doctor routes are restricted to verified `doctor` users. The clinical module spans consultation workload, online meeting links, scheduled and walk-in completion, treatment line items, package invoice creation, active patient programs, medical records, and package catalog management.

dashboard

Workload

Shows confirmed future bookings, active programs originated by the doctor, pending reviews, and clinic schedule.

consultation

Scheduled Completion

Doctors complete only their own confirmed bookings and must provide notes or Slimming Monitoring metrics.

queue

Walk-In Completion

Assigned queue entries move into an in-room workspace and complete into queue-linked consultations.

billing

Line Items

Package options, Diamond add-ons, aesthetic programs, and manual treatments snapshot billing payloads.

programs

Reviews

Weekly progress check-ins become doctor review work and notify patients when feedback is available.

catalog

Packages

Doctors manage global package records through create, update, and deactivate operations.

Workload

Doctor dashboard combines consultations and programs.

The dashboard resolves the authenticated doctor's profile, maps confirmed bookings, active patient programs, pending reviews, and active clinic schedule into a single Inertia payload.

Missing doctor profiles fail with `firstOrFail`; workload rows are filtered by ownership.

Online Booking

Online admin-assisted consultations need a Google Meet link.

Admin-assisted online bookings are confirmed immediately but cannot be completed until the assigned doctor submits a valid Google Meet URL.

The request is locked transactionally and queues a patient or guest notification.

Completion

Scheduled consultations produce notes, metrics, invoices, and follow-up.

Doctors complete only their own confirmed bookings. Completion validates clinical content, creates or updates the consultation, optionally creates a package invoice, stores line items, creates treatment handoffs, completes the booking, and queues follow-up.

Online admin-assisted bookings are blocked until a meeting link exists.

Treatment Billing

Doctor treatment selections snapshot billing facts.

Completion can include primary package options, Diamond oral add-ons, active aesthetic programs, and manual treatment rows. Chargeable rows create internal treatment payments without Midtrans or entitlement activation.

Consultation Line Item Rules

Empty dosage is only a frontend warning; server validation accepts nullable dosage and defaults dosage unit to `ml`.

Queue Consultation

Walk-ins use the same completion workspace without package invoices.

Doctors poll their assigned queue entry, start it, open the workspace, complete clinical content and line items, create treatment handoffs if needed, and mark the queue row completed.

Weekly Follow-Up

Doctor reviews attach feedback to weekly progress rows.

Active programs are scoped through the doctor's source consultations. Weekly progress check-ins with no `reviewed_at` become review tasks; submitting notes updates the same row and queues notification.

Archive

Medical records combine consultations and progress entries.

The archive combines completed consultations and weekly progress check-ins. Consultation records are read-only, while progress records can be updated with metrics, files, notes, and review state.

Catalog

Doctor package management preserves historical links.

Doctors manage the global package catalog through create, update, and deactivate semantics. Deletion is blocked when historical links exist.

Package Catalog Management Flow

Active packages appear in consultation package selection; inactive packages remain linked historically.
